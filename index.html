Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to get my website?

For Basic and Business packages, your website is typically ready within 3-5 business days. Premium packages may take 7-10 days depending on complexity.

Do I need to manage anything after payment?

No! After you make the payment, we handle everything for you. We'll design, develop, and launch your website. You just need to provide your content and we'll take care of the rest.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Absolutely! All our packages include 24/7 support. Business and Premium packages receive priority support with faster response times.

What if I need changes after my website is live?

Premium packages include content changes. For Basic and Business packages, we offer modification services at an additional cost, or you can use the admin panel for Business packages.
